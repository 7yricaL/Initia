 Nutrition Competition 

In collaboration with Back2Basics

Description

In today’s society, the biggest dietary challenge has been to consume nutrient-dense foods, aka healthy foods. Of course, maintaining this lifestyle has become increasingly challenging with the rise of high-fat, glucose-rich, and processed fast foods that tend to be less expensive and more convenient than healthier alternatives.

To counter this challenge, Initia is partnering with Niti Shah, a master's degree holder who also serves as the CEO of Back2Basics. Should your competition entry demonstrate expertise and exceed our expectations, there is a chance your piece could be featured on her reputed nutrition website. We challenge you to enhance the public’s general awareness of nutritional advantages through an innovative program, either in the form of a written blog or video.  

Task

Write a blog/recpie containing a detailed exposition of how to structure your meal, or come up with a healthy nutrient-dense recipe—whether it’s a breakfast, lunch, dinner, or dessert item. The item must be both appetizing and healthy. A team will only pick one of these topics and write a well-developed piece. 

Submission

Competitors are to write a  blog/recipe on the given prompt. This written piece must be a minimum of 2000 words. Competitors also have the option of submitting a supplemental video (optional) as well as the paper.

Awards

 
1st Place - 50 Service Hours + Potential feature on the Back2Basics Website

2nd Place - 45 Service Hours
3rd Place - 40 Service Hours
4th Place - 30 Service Hours
5th Place - 20 Service Hours

Team

Submissions are to be done as a team. Each team will consist of 2-6 members. 

Judges

Submissions will be judged by Niti Shah. With her background in Nutrition, she'll handpick the best submissions.

Guidelines & Rubric

Submissions are to follow the Rules & Guidelines, and will be judged according to This Rubric.

The Grand prize is your blog being published on Niti Shah’s website, which is her decision. Thus having a well-made product increases your chances of being posted on her website. Any five of our winners may have the chance of being posted on her blog.
